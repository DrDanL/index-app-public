Welcome

InDEx is a project funded by the Medical Research Council to develop an alcohol intervention delivered via a hybrid web and mobile app. Our aim is to help people leaving the UK Armed Forces to reduce their drinking and to understand more about their patterns of drinking.

To enable us to conduct our research we will need you to complete a secure online registration process. This will generate a account that is unique to you.
